About Us
There are no rules, just have fun!

More of a toy space than a soft play, you will love that Pixie Play is spotlessly clean and organized (perhaps because it’s run by a team of mamas!).

There’s a ball pool, soft play for babies and toddlers, and more educational toys to complement the usual selection of plastic bits and bobs, along with costumes for dress-up and a great play kitchen. Good for very little ones as it’s quite peaceful.

We believe that play is a valuable learning tool.The place is designed to provide children under 7 years old (or less than 130cm in height) an imaginative, challenging, and fun yet safe environment to play and explore.

We are constantly looking for ways to improve our services and will be developing classes, special activities, and more. We appreciate your feedback as we try our best in developing a unique place space for children and grown-ups alike

Safety and hygiene are our utmost concerns and they will never be compromised. After all, we, ourselves are our biggest critic. It is our greatest joy to realise a dream haven for our children.
